Hello, I’m
Koh Tze Rui.

I’m a student at the National University of Singapore who loves High Performance Computing, or anything that makes a process run really fast.

A little context

I work between understanding and application.

My practice blends research, understanding, and writing. I enjoy creating visual representations of complex HPC applications and concepts, making what I learn more digestible for technical audiences who share my curiosity.

“If you want to master something, teach it.”

— Richard Feynman

This site is both my portfolio and mental notebook. Finished work lives beside the messy thinking that made it possible, together with meticulously curated diagrams and explanations, because the path is often the most reusable part.

I hope to master this field someday, and I invite the people reading this to follow the journey.
